FORMAG AS THE PART OF GTI Ltd

Formag is the part of GTI. Global Transport Investments (GTI) is a parent company for many freight forwarding companies and liner agencies. It incorporates 19 transport companies throughout the world. Being an individual member of FIATA and Multiport Association it constantly grows and broadens horizons for the companies of the group. The company holds the leading positions in the market of freight forwarding for several decades. The key to success for Formag is sustainable development and long-term and mutually beneficial relations with customers.
