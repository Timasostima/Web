Midnight Carousel

An unexpected traffic jam turned magical when a shipment of carousel horses bound for a new amusement park spilled onto the highway near La Ciudad de los Caballos. The intricately carved horses, illuminated by the moonlight, seemed to come to life, casting a spell over the stranded motorists. The incident, occurring just before the stroke of midnight, transformed the mundane into the extraordinary. The local authorities, charmed by the scene, organized a temporary carousel for the public to enjoy while the cleanup took place. The event has inspired the town to host a yearly midnight festival celebrating the enchanting night when carousel horses danced under the stars.
