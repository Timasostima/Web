Skyward Surprise

The town of Pato Feliz experienced a bizarre and delightful incident when thousands of rubber ducks descended upon its central square. An air cargo plane en route to a novelty store encountered technical difficulties, resulting in the accidental release of its cargo. The sky filled with yellow as the ducks fell, creating a surreal and festive atmosphere. The townspeople quickly turned the mishap into a celebration, with local businesses offering duck-themed promotions and the town council instituting an annual ‘Duck Day.’ The event has since become a cherished local tradition, symbolizing the community’s ability to find joy in the unexpected.
